⚔️ UNDERSTANDING CYBER ATTACKS

🎣 PHISHING ATTACKS

Deceptive emails and messages that steal your credentials.

⚠️ HOW HACKERS DO IT: Attackers impersonate trusted organizations (banks, social media, government). They send urgent emails with malicious links or attachments that look legitimate.

🎯 WHAT THEY WANT:

  • Login credentials (passwords, usernames)
  • Credit card numbers and bank details
  • Personal identification information (SSN, Aadhar, PAN)
  • Access to your email and social media accounts

🛡️ HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF:

  • ✅ Always check the sender's email address carefully (look for misspellings)
  • ✅ Hover over links before clicking to see the real URL
  • ✅ Never share passwords, OTPs, or personal info via email
  • ✅ Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) on all accounts
  • ✅ Look for spelling mistakes and urgent language in emails
  • ✅ Report suspicious emails to your security team
  • ✅ Use email filtering and spam protection

📊 90% of cyber attacks start with a phishing email!

🐴 TROJAN HORSE

Malware disguised as legitimate software.

⚠️ HOW HACKERS DO IT: Hackers hide malicious code inside seemingly innocent files - games, PDFs, software installers, email attachments, or fake updates.

🎯 WHAT TROJANS DO:

  • Steal passwords and login credentials
  • Record keystrokes (keylogging)
  • Take screenshots and spy on your activity
  • Give hackers remote access to your computer
  • Install additional malware without your knowledge
  • Use your computer to send spam or attack others

🛡️ HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF:

  • ✅ Only download software from official websites
  • ✅ Never open attachments from unknown senders
  • ✅ Keep antivirus software updated
  • ✅ Use a firewall to monitor suspicious connections
  • ✅ Be cautious of "free" software or cracked versions
  • ✅ Scan downloaded files before opening
  • ✅ Enable real-time protection in your antivirus

📊 Free software downloads are the #1 source of Trojans!

💀 RANSOMWARE

Malware that encrypts your files and demands payment.

⚠️ HOW HACKERS DO IT: Through phishing emails, malicious ads, drive-by downloads, or exploiting outdated software vulnerabilities.

🎯 WHAT RANSOMWARE DOES:

  • Encrypts all your files (photos, documents, videos)
  • Demands ransom payment in cryptocurrency
  • Threatens to delete files if not paid
  • Can spread across networks to infect others
  • Often targets businesses and hospitals

🛡️ HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF:

  • ✅ Regularly backup important files to external drives or cloud
